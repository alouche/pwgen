What is pwgen.

pwgen is a small firefox addon which enables at the click of the finger, secure password generation. It is currently used by over 5000 users with 99k+ downloads

How To

1. Once you installed pwgen... an icon will show up in your firefox statusbar.
2. Click on it, and a password will be generated using the default settings. By default, the password will be displayed and directly copied in your clipboard.
3. Right click on the icon and click on "Options..." to change your settings.
